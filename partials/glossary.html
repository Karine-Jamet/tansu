Glossary

If you begin in the kimono field you may be a bit lost on your tansu. Here is a glossary to help you identified obi or kimono styles and motifs

Kimono

Tomesode: One principale color and motif only on the bottom Furisode : Long sleeves Houmongi : Motif on bottom sleeves and shoulder Iromuji : Non motif, one color Komon : Little motif repeated Yukata : Casual summer kimono Kakeshita : Weeding coat wear above kimono Uchikake : Weeding kimono

Obi

Maru Fukuro Nagoya Hanhaba Kaku Heko

Kistuke

Juban Haneri Koshihimo Ohashori Otaiko Yuzen Shibori Hakama
